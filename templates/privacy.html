Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information you provide directly to us. This includes your email address when you create an account, and any expense data you choose to log such as amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We also collect basic usage information like when and how you interact with our service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. Your expense information is used to generate your personal spending summaries, category breakdowns, and financial insights. We use your email address for account-related communications, service updates, and security notifications.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption and access controls. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your expense data is private and accessible only to you. We may share aggregated, anonymized usage statistics for analytical purposes, but this data cannot be used to identify you personally.

5. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, or communication purposes. These third parties have access to your information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated to protect your information. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party services we integrate with.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to ensure the proper functioning of our service. These cookies are necessary for authentication and security purposes. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking purposes across other websites.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can export all your expense data or delete your account entirely through your account settings. If you have any requests regarding your data, please contact us at support@spendly.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Spendly is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@spendly.com.
